Cancellation & Refund Policy

General Policy

At DPS Sports Complex, we strive to provide excellent service to all our customers. This Cancellation & Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding cancellations and refunds for services booked through our platform.

By making a booking with us, you agree to the terms of this policy. Please read this policy carefully before making any bookings.

Cancellation by Customers

Sports Sessions & Events

  • Cancellations made more than 48 hours before the scheduled session/event will receive a full refund minus any applicable payment processing fees.
  • Cancellations made between 24-48 hours before the scheduled session/event will receive a 50% refund of the booking amount.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled session/event are non-refundable.
  • No-shows will be charged the full amount and no refund will be provided.

Membership Plans

  • Monthly memberships can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. No refund will be provided for the current billing cycle.
  • Annual memberships can be cancelled within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. After 30 days, cancellations will be prorated based on unused months minus a 15% cancellation fee.
  • Special promotional memberships are non-refundable.

Special Events & Tournaments

  • Special events and tournaments may have specific cancellation policies that will be clearly stated at the time of booking.
  • Generally, these bookings are non-refundable unless the event is cancelled by DPS Sports Complex.

Cancellation by DPS Sports Complex

In the rare event that DPS Sports Complex needs to cancel a session, event, or facility booking:

  • Customers will be notified immediately via email and/or SMS.
  • A full refund will be processed automatically within 5-7 business days.
  • Alternatively, customers may choose to receive a credit voucher for future bookings (valid for 6 months).

Cancellations may occur due to:

  • Inclement weather (for outdoor facilities)
  • Facility maintenance or repairs
  • Insufficient bookings
  • Other unforeseen circumstances

Refund Process

Eligibility

Refunds will only be issued according to the terms outlined in this policy. To be eligible for a refund:

  • Cancellation must be made through our official channels (website, mobile app, or email).
  • The request must be made within the specified time frames.
  • The original payment receipt must be provided.

Processing Time

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days from the date of cancellation approval.
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the booking.
  • For bank transfers, processing may take additional 2-3 business days depending on your bank.

Deductions

All refunds are subject to:

  • Any applicable cancellation fees as per this policy
  • Payment gateway/processing fees (typically 2-3% of transaction amount)
  • Taxes as applicable by law

Rescheduling Policy

As an alternative to cancellation, you may reschedule your booking subject to availability:

  • Rescheduling requests must be made at least 24 hours before the original booking time.
  • Only one reschedule is allowed per booking.
  • The rescheduled session must occur within 30 days of the original booking date.
  • Rescheduling is not available for special events or tournaments.

To reschedule, please contact our customer support at support@dpssportscomplex.com or through your account on our website/mobile app.

Special Circumstances

Medical Emergencies

In case of genuine medical emergencies (supported by valid medical documentation):

  • Full refunds may be granted at the discretion of management.
  • Requests must be submitted within 7 days of the incident.
  • Documentation must be provided within 14 days of the request.

Government Restrictions

If government regulations prevent the use of our facilities:

  • All affected bookings will be automatically cancelled.
  • Customers may choose between a full refund or credit voucher (valid for 12 months).
